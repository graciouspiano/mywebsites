Hardware

The Hardware in a Computer
The Motherboard The Motherboard is one of the most vital parts in a motherboard. The Motherboard contains the cpu slot, the ram stick slots, and the usb's and the processor. What the motherboard does, is that it takes all the data, and process it, then continues to send information to the other parts in the computer. It is pretty much the brain.
The CPUs The CPU is the processor, which processes the data that goes to the motherboard.
Ram Sticks The ram sticks are one of the important parts. If you do not have enough ram, your computer will constantly crash, and you wouldn't be able to run certain program. The ram is the amount of thinking power that your computer has, and the amount of multitasking power that it has.
Video Card The video card is a pretty important part of the computer. If you would like to run graphicly intensive programs, then you will need a good card. The graphics card supports the graphics that the computer can handle. The card can have certain amount of vram, which is the memory of the card. you can have 256 megabytes, 512 megabytes, 1024 megabytes, 2048 megabytes, 4048 megabytes.
A monitor! A monitor is a screen. The screen is what projects everything. Mintors differ in graphics, for example there are monitors which are 1920x1080 pixels, 1680x920 pixels, and 2560-1440 pixels. Now there's new screens that are 3840x2160 pixels
Fans The fans are fans that cool the items in the computer, therefore causing the computer not to blow up. Quite vital items.
Power supply The power supply is the little box that gives your computer electricity, to make everything work in your computer.
